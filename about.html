Our Story

Founded in 2021, Jelabakes is a home-based baking business that brings a touch of love and creativity to every baked product. Founded by the dynamic duo of Jessica Laura (Jela Sy) and Jersey Diaz, Jelabakes has quickly become a go-to source for delicious, high-quality treats. Together, they combine their strengths—Jela’s baking expertise and social media savvy with Jersey’s business acumen—to offer customers not only mouthwatering pastries but also great value.

Jelabakes' Core Principles

✔ Quality & Affordability: Every product is made with the finest ingredients while maintaining fair and accessible pricing.

✔ Customer Experience: Baking is more than a transaction; it’s an experience filled with warmth and passion.

✔ Personalized Service: Every order, whether big or small, is treated with care and attention to make customers feel valued.

✔ Consistency & Excellence: Commitment to delivering top-tier products and exceptional service every time.

✔ Building Connections: Creating lasting relationships with customers through trust, passion, and quality.
